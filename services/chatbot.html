Local Chatbot & BM25 Knowledge Base

The floating chatbot searches a bilingual JSONL corpus with a lightweight BM25 ranker. Results stay on-device, filtered by the visitor’s selected language (EN/ES).

BM25 Retrieval Bilingual Responses Secure & Local Service-Aware

Essence of the Chatbot

How it works

  • Embeds ops_bm25_corpus.jsonl directly in the page and parses it on load.
  • Tokenizes titles, bodies, and tags to construct inverse document frequencies.
  • Ranks documents locally, highlighting matched snippets and CTA badges.
  • Links visitors to anchor targets inside the service sections for faster wayfinding.

Security & Compliance Notes

The chatbot honors the OPS CyberSec Core approach: no third-party calls, adherence to the site CSP, and data retention messaging aligned with the 90-day policy. Visitors remain in control of their information footprint.

Using the Chatbot

Steps

  1. Select English or Spanish using the global toggle in the main interface.
  2. Open the floating Chatbot button to reveal the search panel.
  3. Ask about roles, services, logistics, or SLAs and review the top-ranked snippets.
  4. Jump directly to a service section using the provided anchor link.
